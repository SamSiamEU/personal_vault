# Acqura GTM Tier Architecture
**Date:** 2026-04-21
**Status:** Decision confirmed
**Context:** Bullish/bearish projection analysis revealed need for multiple revenue streams beyond Eksido OSP delivery alone.

---

## Overview

Acqura operates across three GTM tiers. These are not product packages — they are go-to-market entry points that reflect the client's current maturity, stack, and buying conversation. Each tier has a natural graduation path to the next.

The critical insight: Tier 1 requires no Salesforce. This opens Acqura to thousands of Indian D2C brands on Braze, MoEngage, and WebEngage that would never consider a platform migration conversation.

---

## Tier 1 — Intelligence Only ("Analytics in a Box")

**What it is:** TIP taxonomy implementation, Campaign ID governance, UTM discipline, BigQuery measurement stack, Growth Pulse dashboard. Works on top of any existing tool — MoEngage, WebEngage, Braze, or any ESP/CDP. No SFMC required.

**Who it's for:** Phase 1-2 brands that can't see clearly what their existing media spend is doing. CFO/CMO conversation, not a platform migration conversation.

**Entry barrier:** Low
**Implementation:** 2-3 weeks
**Sales cycle:** Short
**Pricing:** €800-1,500/month
**Margin:** 70-75%

**Why Tier 1 changes everything:**
Every brand on Braze, MoEngage, WebEngage in Indian D2C is a potential Tier 1 client without ever touching SFMC. The conversation is not "migrate your platform" — it is "let us show you what your existing spend is actually doing." Clarity sells the rest.

**Graduation trigger:** Once the intelligence layer reveals that lifecycle marketing is underperforming, the argument for Tier 2 activation makes itself. You don't sell SFMC. You sell clarity.

---

## Tier 2 — Intelligence + Activation ("Acqura Growth OS, Shared")

**What it is:** Everything in Tier 1 plus full SFMC OSP delivery — lifecycle journeys, WhatsApp, email automation, standardised delivery on shared MC Engage Corporate infrastructure.

**Who it's for:** Tier 1 graduates ready to activate on their intelligence. Founder-to-founder, warm referral entry.

**Entry barrier:** Medium
**Implementation:** 3-4 weeks
**Sales cycle:** Medium — warm referral preferred
**Pricing:** €2,000-2,500/month
**Margin:** 40-50% at 8+ clients

**Graduation trigger:** Dedicated infrastructure requirements, scale, or Agentforce activation needs push client to Tier 3. No re-architecture required — Service Cloud org already in place from day one.

---

## Tier 3 — Full Stack ("Acqura Growth OS, Dedicated")

**What it is:** Everything in Tier 2 plus dedicated MC instance, MCNext-ready, full Agentforce activation, MCI integration, executive ROAS reporting.

**Who it's for:** Tier 2 graduates at scale, or enterprise clients entering directly at full stack.

**Entry barrier:** High
**Pricing:** €4,000-8,000/month
**Margin:** 55-65% (shared infrastructure costs already absorbed by Tier 1/2 base)

---

## The Graduation Path

Graduation is not a sales pitch — it is a natural consequence of what the intelligence layer reveals. Each tier creates the conditions for the next conversation.

**Tier 1 → Tier 2**
The Growth Pulse dashboard shows the client exactly where their lifecycle marketing is failing. Abandoned cart sequences are underperforming. Win-back campaigns don't exist. RTO-adjusted ROAS reveals which campaigns are generating low-intent buyers. The data makes the Tier 2 argument — Acqura does not have to. Trigger: 60–90 days of intelligence delivery, one clear lifecycle gap identified and quantified.

**Tier 2 → Tier 3**
The shared infrastructure ceiling becomes visible at scale. Multiple brands in the same MC Engage Corporate instance creates sendability constraints. Agentforce activation, MCI integration, or executive-level reporting requirements exceed what a shared environment can support cleanly. The client has already proven willingness to pay and trust in delivery. Trigger: >50k active contacts, Agentforce requirement, or dedicated compliance/data isolation need.

**Direct Tier 3 entry**
Enterprise clients or Eksido OSP graduates who already have the intelligence maturity and scale may bypass Tier 1 and 2 entirely. These are not cold sales — they come through the Eksido relationship or Dennis's Merkle network. Entry at Tier 3 without a warm relationship is unlikely.

```
[Tier 1] Analytics in a Box
    €800–1,500/month · Any stack · 2–3 weeks

[Tier 2] Intelligence + Activation
    €2,000–2,500/month · SFMC shared · 3–4 weeks
         ↓  (scale, Agentforce, or isolation need)
[Tier 3] Full Stack Dedicated
    €4,000–8,000/month · Dedicated MC · enterprise
```

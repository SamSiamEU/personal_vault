## Current Thesis

*Too early — 1 source ingested. Thesis will form at 3-5 sources.*

Early signal: the paid media measurement stack is fragmented and brittle at the data layer. Marketers running multi-channel campaigns face structural gaps in their reporting — non-Google cost data doesn't flow into GA4 without specific UTM hygiene, and the native solution (GA4 Cost Data Import) is manual and error-prone. A category of point solutions (OWOX, likely others) has formed around patching these gaps. Acqura's opportunity may lie above this layer — not patching plumbing, but providing intelligence on top of it.
